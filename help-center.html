Help Center & FAQ

Profile

Getting Started

How do I create an account?

Click the "Sign Up" button on the homepage, fill in your details, and verify your email address. You'll then have access to all NeuroVytis features.

What data do I need to provide?

Basic personal information, medical history, and optional brain scan data. All data is encrypted and HIPAA-compliant.

Using the Platform

How do I upload my brain scans?

Go to the "Upload Data" section, drag and drop your EEG/MRI files, and our AI will process them automatically.

What do the quiz results mean?

Quiz results provide a cognitive assessment score. Higher scores indicate better cognitive function. Results are compared to age-matched norms.

Privacy & Security

Is my data secure?

Yes. We use end-to-end encryption, HIPAA compliance, and regular security audits to protect your data.

Can I delete my account?

Yes. Go to Settings and select "Delete Account". Your data will be permanently removed within 30 days.
