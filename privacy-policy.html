Privacy Policy

Profile

1. Introduction

NeuroVytis ("Company", "we", "our", "us") operates as a HIPAA-compliant healthcare analytics platform. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our service.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly, including:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, date of birth)
  • Medical and health data (cognitive test results, brain scan data)
  • Biometric data (EEG, MRI scans, genetic information)
  • Lifestyle information (diet, exercise, sleep patterns)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and improve our services
  • Conduct AI-powered risk assessments
  • Generate personalized health recommendations
  • Support research and development
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Data Protection

Your data is protected through:

  • End-to-end encryption
  • HIPAA compliance
  • Secure servers with advanced firewalls
  • Regular security audits

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request data correction
  • Request data deletion
  • Opt-out of data sharing

6. Contact Us

For privacy concerns, contact: privacy@neurovytis.com
