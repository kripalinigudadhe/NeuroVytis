Alzheimer's Prevention Tips

Profile

🏃 Stay Physically Active

  • Exercise 150 minutes per week
  • Include aerobic and strength training
  • Walking is an excellent starting point
  • Improves blood flow to the brain

🥗 Eat a Healthy Diet

  • Follow Mediterranean diet principles
  • Eat fish rich in omega-3 fatty acids
  • Include berries and leafy greens
  • Limit processed foods and sugar

😴 Prioritize Sleep

  • Aim for 7-9 hours nightly
  • Maintain consistent sleep schedule
  • Create a dark, cool bedroom
  • Avoid screens before bedtime

🧠 Keep Your Mind Active

  • Learn new skills and languages
  • Solve puzzles and play games
  • Read regularly
  • Engage in creative activities

👥 Stay Socially Connected

  • Maintain regular social interactions
  • Join clubs or community groups
  • Spend time with family and friends
  • Volunteer in your community

🧘 Manage Stress

  • Practice meditation or yoga
  • Manage work-life balance
  • Practice deep breathing exercises
  • Seek professional help if needed

🏥 Regular Health Check-ups

  • Monitor blood pressure and cholesterol
  • Manage diabetes if present
  • Regular cognitive assessments
  • Discuss family history with doctor

🚫 Avoid Risk Factors

  • Don't smoke
  • Limit alcohol consumption
  • Avoid head injuries
  • Protect hearing health
